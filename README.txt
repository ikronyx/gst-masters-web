## Project Overview

GST Interior Design Masters | Private Limited is a professional interior design company that transforms spaces into **beautiful, functional works of art**. Our services cater to both residential and commercial clients, offering tailored solutions to enhance your lifestyle and workspace.

The website showcases our **services, process, philosophy, portfolio, testimonials, and contact information**, designed with a modern, sleek, and elegant UI.

---

## Features

* **Responsive Design:** Fully compatible with desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
* **Hero Section:** Engaging banner highlighting our main services and brand message.
* **Services Section:** Residential, commercial, home staging, and renovation offerings presented in modern cards with icons.
* **Our Process Section:** Step-by-step explanation of how projects are executed from consultation to final reveal.
* **Philosophy Section:** Highlights our core design values, including creativity, functionality, and sustainability.
* **Testimonials Section:** Slider to showcase client feedback and success stories.
* **Blog Section:** Display of recent blog posts with thumbnails and meta information.
* **Contact Section:** Company contact info, newsletter subscription, and Google Maps integration if needed.
* **Footer:** Company details, social links, and copyright information.

## Installation & Usage

1. Clone or download the repository.
2. Open the `index.html` file in your web browser.
3. Ensure all images and assets are in the correct `/images` folder.
4. Customize content, services, and contact details as needed.
